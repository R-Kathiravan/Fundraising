How It Works

Starting a fundraiser or donating is simple. In just a few steps, you can bring hope, raise funds, and make a real difference.

The Three-Step Process (For Fundraisers)

1. Create Your Fundraiser

Tell your story in your own words. Set your goal, add photos or a video, and provide your details. It takes less than 5 minutes to set up your campaign page, completely free.

2. Share Your Story

Your campaign is ready to share! Send your unique link to friends and family through WhatsApp, Facebook, Instagram, and email. Your community is your biggest strength.

3. Receive Funds Directly

Donations are transferred securely and directly to your registered bank account via UPI or bank transfer. Withdraw your funds at any time, even if you haven't reached your goal.

Our Promise

0% Platform Fee

You keep every rupee you raise. We never charge a platform fee. Only standard payment gateway charges apply.

Secure Indian Payments

We accept all major payment methods including UPI, Cards, and Netbanking via our secure gateway.

Easy Fund Withdrawal

No need to wait to reach your goal. Withdraw your funds directly to your bank account at any time.

Dedicated Support

Our expert support team is available 24/7 via chat and email to help you with your campaign.
